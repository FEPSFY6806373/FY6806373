Conference paper: Trasforming fiction into practice, a new alternative to iron domes

Abstract

  The unfortunate growth of wars throughout the years, have led to necessary improvements in the attack or defense systems of countries.
  Using the current fundamentals behind the air defense system of iron domes, combined with a fictional background, an alternative
  proposal is introduced. The collaboration between the reality of the existing iron dome and the fiction of the Korean drama ‘Gird’ 
  desires the safety of people, their homes, environment and ultimately their country.
